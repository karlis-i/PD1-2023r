The Greatest Estate Developer

The Greatest Estate Developer is a Korean webnovel written by BK_Moon and ilustrated by Manggi. It was serialized on NAVER Series from June 3rd, 2019 to November 12th, 2020. A manhwa adaptation adapted by Lee Hyunmin and illustrated by Kim Hyunsoo began on August 5th, 2021 on NAVER Webtoon.

Main characters

Lloyd

Lloyd Frontera is a leading construction engineer responsible for the development of the Frontera County. He is the eldest son of Arcos and Marbella Frontera, the older brother of Julian Frontera, and the main protagonist of The Greatest Estate Developer. His body is currently inhabited by Suho Kim. The fate of his original soul is unknown.
Lloyd has a rather malleable face and can be seen making various, severely exaggerated expressions much to the surprise, disgust, and horror of those around him. These expressions have led him to be called a monster, demon, and thug amongst other names.

Javier

Javier Asrahan is a loyal knight of the Frontera family. He is the protagonist of Knight of Blood and Iron and the deuteragonist of The Greatest Estate Developer. He currently acts as the personal guard of Lloyd Frontera and accompanies him on nearly every occasion.

The Queen

Alicia Termina Magentano is the reigning empress currently residing in the royal capital of Magenta. She holds the highest authority in the land as well as absolute power with her overwhelming charisma, intelligence, and strength.
